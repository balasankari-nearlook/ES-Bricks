Work Process

How It Work

An overview of how the process unfolds, from gathering raw materials to delivering the final product.

Get In Touch

STEP -01

Mixing

Raw materials like clay and water are combined to form a homogenous mixture.

STEP -02

Molding

The mixed clay is shaped into bricks using molds or machines, ensuring uniformity.

STEP -03

Drying

The formed bricks are dried either naturally in the sun or through artificial drying methods.

STEP -04

Firing

Dried bricks are fired in kilns at high temperatures to harden and strengthen them, making them ready for use in construction.
